About PathFinder 10

PathFinder 10 is an interactive, immersive 3D Career Navigator designed specifically for 10th-grade students in Telangana, India. Transitioning from high school is a critical turning point, and our goal is to empower students with data-driven, localized information and personalized AI guidance to make confident educational decisions.

⚡

Personalized Quiz

Our 10-question interest profiler evaluates core affinities and aligns students with the three major pathways in Telangana.

📊

Deep-Dive Matrix

Side-by-side technical evaluation of parameters like cost, exam stages (TS POLYCET, TS ECET), salary growth, and job sector splits.

🤖

Gemini AI Advisor

A state-of-the-art conversational guide leveraging Secure AI to answer queries about specific colleges, syllabus maps, and recruitment pathways.

Educational Pathways Mapped

Intermediate Academics (2 Years): Traditional general academic streams including MPC (Engineering), BiPC (Medical), CEC (Commerce), and HEC (Arts), preparing students for entrance exams like JEE, NEET, and TG EAPCET.
Polytechnic Diploma (3 Years): Applied engineering streams giving students hands-on technical training via TS POLYCET and a direct lateral entry path to B.Tech 2nd year via TS ECET.
ITI Vocational Trades (1-2 Years): Rapid placement-oriented training in electrical, fitting, and computer trades, leading to immediate employment opportunities in state divisions (TGSPDCL, SCCL) and Indian Railways (Assistant Loco Pilot).
