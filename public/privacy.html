🛡️ User Data & Protection

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

PathFinder 10 collects minimal information to provide tailored career counseling services for 10th-grade students in Telangana. The data collected includes:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, and authentication credentials via Firebase Auth.
  • Quiz Profiles: Answers to career assessment questions and score logs.
  • Guest Logs: Simulated session data stored locally on your device's browser (Local Storage).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is processed strictly to:

  • Generate personalized pathway recommendations (Intermediate, Polytechnic, ITI).
  • Maintain user sessions and save assessment milestones.
  • Power custom Gemini AI advising recommendations based on your preferences.

3. Data Security & Storage

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Firebase credentials and cloud database items are secured through official Firebase Web SDK rules.
  • We do not share, sell, or rent student details to third-party marketing networks.
  • Under Guest Mode, all data is retained 100% locally on your machine and can be cleared instantly.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize secure third-party services including Google Firebase (authentication and hosting config) and the Google Gemini AI proxy. These partners treat your data in strict compliance with their respective privacy standards.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or data removal requests regarding this privacy statement, please submit your inquiries via our Contact Us portal.
