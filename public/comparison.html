⏱️ Course Duration
Intermediate 2 Years

Standard 2-year academic program split into Junior and Senior Intermediate years.

Polytechnic Diploma 3 Years

Full-time 3-year technical program. Incorporates rigorous practical semesters.

ITI Trades 1 to 2 Years

Vocational trades (e.g. Welder is 1 Year, Electrician & Fitter are 2 Years).

📝 Admission & Exams
Intermediate Direct Merit-based

Admission directly into junior colleges based on 10th standard GPA/GPA marks. No state entrance exams needed for entry.

Polytechnic Diploma TS POLYCET Exam

Requires writing the state-level **TS POLYCET** entrance exam. Seats allocated based on exam rank counselor list.

ITI Trades Direct Merit Counselling

Direct entry. Admission is handled via state counseling processes based solely on 10th-grade board exam scores.

💰 Cost & Subsidies
Intermediate Varies Heavily

Extremely low in Gov colleges, but private corporate academies charges high tuition fees (₹50k - ₹1.5L per year).

Polytechnic Diploma Highly Subsidized

Government fee structures are heavily subsidized (₹3,000 - ₹5,000/year). State scholarships available for eligible ranks.

ITI Trades Absolute Lowest Cost

Very minimal fees. Government ITI centers offer student stipends and free tool kit benefits during training.

🔬 Practical Balance
Intermediate 80% Theory / 20% Lab

Heavy emphasis on mathematical proofs, analytical scientific theory, and written assessments. Minimal lab experiments.

Polytechnic Diploma 50% Theory / 50% Lab

Perfect balance. Classroom theory is immediately backed by structural laboratory validation and workshop projects.

ITI Trades 20% Theory / 80% Workshop

Almost entirely vocational. Intensive hands-on machinery training, plant operation drills, and tool safety workshops.

🚀 B.Tech Engineering Path
Intermediate Full 4-Year B.Tech

Requires writing the **TG EAPCET** (or JEE) to get engineering seats. Complete a standard 4-year undergraduate syllabus.

Polytechnic Diploma 3-Year B.Tech (Lateral Entry)

Can write the **TS ECET** exam to gain direct **Lateral Entry admission** into the 2nd year of B.Tech, skipping the first year.

ITI Trades Diploma Required First

Must clear the ITI-to-Diploma bridge examinations first, or gain 2-year industrial experience, before lateral entry.

💼 Job Market Outlook
Intermediate Degree Required First

Almost zero direct employment pathways. Requires completing graduation (B.Tech, BSc, CA, MBBS) to sit for job recruitments.

Polytechnic Diploma Junior Engineer (JE)

Immediate qualification for Junior Engineer posts in **TGSPDCL**, Transco, TSGENCO, SSC, and Indian Railways.

ITI Trades Technician / ALP

Immediate eligibility for Indian Railways **Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP)**, state electricity board line-technicians, and workshops.
